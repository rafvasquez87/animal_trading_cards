Killer Whale

The killer whale is not a fish. It is a marine mammal. However, it is not a whale. It is in fact a dolphin, and the largest dolphin to boot.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Average Length: 27 feet
  • Average Lifespan: Male 17y Female 29y
  • Habitat: All Oceans

Size relative to a bus: Orcas, or killer whales, are the largest of the dolphins and one of the world's most powerful predators. They feast on marine mammals such as seals, sea lions, and even whales, employing teeth that can be four inches (ten centimeters) long. They are known to grab seals right off the ice.
